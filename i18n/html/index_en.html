Plugin for downloading cadastral data for Navarra (Spain)


QGIS plugin developed by the GIS and Development Area

of ​​the Geoinnova Association to download graphic and

alphanumeric cadastral data from the Navarra (Spain).


The download uses the url available on the website of the

Navarra Territorial Wealth and Patrimonial Tax Service (SRT).


Más información y contacto en info@geoinnova.org
